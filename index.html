Pixels to Patients (P2P-CV)

Bridging CV State-of-the-Art with Clinical Impact — WACV 2026 Workshop

Call for Papers (CFP)

We invite contributions presenting core computer-vision innovations that translate effectively into clinical practice — bridging algorithmic methods and deployment challenges to make insights relevant for both medical AI and the broader CV community.

Topics of Interest

  • Foundation models for healthcare (pretraining, fine-tuning, adaptation)
  • Vision–language learning for decision support and reporting
  • Domain shift and generalization across scanners, sites, and populations
  • Deployment: MLOps, continual learning, monitoring, and regulation
  • Trustworthy and interpretable AI; safety-critical evaluation
  • Fairness and bias auditing in heterogeneous cohorts
  • Data efficiency: self-supervised, active, or weak supervision
  • Quantitative biomarkers and prognosis modeling
  • Human–AI collaboration; clinician-in-the-loop systems

These topics align with the officially accepted WACV 2026 proposal scope.

Submission Instructions

Tracks

  1. Regular Papers (≤ 8 pages): archival, double-blind; highlight both CV contribution and clinical translation.
  2. Extended Abstracts (2–4 pages): non-archival; works-in-progress or pilot/negative results.
  3. Problem-Pitch Proposals (2 slides): non-archival; reviewed by organizers and presented live to seed collaborations.

Proceedings & Policy

  • Accepted papers will appear in the official WACV 2026 workshop proceedings.
  • All presenting authors must register for the workshop.
