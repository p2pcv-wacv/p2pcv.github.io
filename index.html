Pixels to Patients (P2P-CV)

Bridging CV State-of-the-Art with Clinical Impact — WACV 2026 Workshop

Call for Papers (CFP)

We invite contributions presenting cutting-edge CV innovations that translate effectively into clinical practice — bridging algorithmic methods and deployment challenges.

  • Foundation models for healthcare
  • Vision–language learning for decision support
  • Domain shift & generalization
  • Deployment & monitoring in clinical workflows
  • Trustworthy, interpretable & fair AI
  • Data efficiency (SSL, active, weak supervision)
  • Quantitative biomarkers & prognosis
  • Human–AI collaboration

Submission Instructions

  1. Regular Papers (≤ 8 pages): archival, double-blind.
  2. Extended Abstracts (2–4 pages): non-archival, works-in-progress.
  3. Problem-Pitch Proposals (2 slides): non-archival, live discussion.

Follow the WACV 2026 author kit for formatting; templates will be posted once released.
