About the Workshop

Pixels to Patients (P2P-CV) is a full-day workshop dedicated to bridging the gap between computer-vision research and its safe, effective deployment in healthcare. It addresses the complete pipeline—from foundation model training to clinical monitoring and regulation.

  • Foundation models and multimodal learning for medical imaging.
  • Domain generalization and cross-institution robustness.
  • Trustworthy, explainable, and fair AI for clinical deployment.
  • Human–AI collaboration and continual learning in healthcare systems.
