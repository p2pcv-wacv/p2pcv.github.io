Pixels to Patients (P2P-CV)

Bridging CV State-of-the-Art with Clinical Impact — WACV 2026 Workshop

Call for Papers (CFP)

We invite contributions that present core CV innovations and critically address their translation into clinical practice — bridging methods and deployment to make lessons relevant for both medical AI and the broader CV community. [oai_citation:1‡WACF___Workshop_detailed.pdf](file-service://file-CAHhrYsWDmCqPaVJcdQtFz)

Topics of interest

  • Foundation models for healthcare (pretraining, fine-tuning, adaptation)
  • Vision–language learning for decision support & reporting
  • Domain shift & generalization across scanners/sites/populations
  • Deployment: MLOps, continual learning, monitoring, regulation
  • Trustworthy & interpretable AI; safety-critical evaluation
  • Fairness & bias auditing in heterogeneous cohorts
  • Data efficiency: SSL, active/weak supervision
  • Quantitative biomarkers & prognosis
  • Human–AI collaboration; clinician-in-the-loop systems

These tracks and themes mirror our workshop proposal scope. [oai_citation:2‡WACF___Workshop_detailed.pdf](file-service://file-CAHhrYsWDmCqPaVJcdQtFz)
